Buttons

  • (C)lear: clears the grid of any live cells.
  • (P)opulate: randomly populates a third of the grid with live cells.
  • Run (1) cycle: iterates through the 'game' 1 full cycle.
  • (R)un continuous : lets the game run repeatedly through the cycles until either all live cells die or you stop the game.
  • Stop: stops the continuous mode (only appears when continuous is selected).

* Note: short-cut keys for the buttoms are contained within brackets (e.g. 'c' will clear the grid)
* You can also click any of the cells to change their state.
